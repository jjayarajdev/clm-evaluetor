## 1. DEFINITIONS

1.1 **"Affiliate"** means any entity that directly or indirectly controls, is controlled by, or is under common control with a Party.

1.2 **"Confidential Information"** means all non-public information disclosed by either Party, including but not limited to trade secrets, business plans, customer data, technical specifications, and financial information.

1.3 **"Deliverables"** means all work product, documentation, software, and materials provided by Provider under any Statement of Work.

1.4 **"Service Levels"** or **"SLAs"** means the performance standards set forth in Exhibit A attached hereto.

1.5 **"Statement of Work"** or **"SOW"** means a document executed by the Parties describing specific services to be performed.

---

## 2. SCOPE OF SERVICES

2.1 Provider shall provide IT infrastructure management, application support, and helpdesk services as detailed in executed Statements of Work.

2.2 Services shall be performed in accordance with industry best practices and the Service Level Agreements specified in Exhibit A.

---

## 3. TERM AND RENEWAL

3.1 **Initial Term:** This Agreement shall commence on the Effective Date and continue for three (3) years ("Initial Term").

3.2 **Auto-Renewal:** This Agreement shall automatically renew for successive one (1) year periods unless either Party provides written notice of non-renewal at least ninety (90) days prior to the expiration of the then-current term.

3.3 **Notice Period:** Either Party must provide ninety (90) days written notice to terminate this Agreement.

---

## 4. FEES AND PAYMENT

4.1 **Base Monthly Fee:** Client shall pay Provider a base monthly fee of **$125,000.00 USD** for the services described herein.

4.2 **Total Contract Value:** The total estimated value of this Agreement is **$4,500,000.00 USD** over the Initial Term.

4.3 **Payment Terms:** All invoices are due and payable within **Net 30** days of receipt.

4.4 **Late Payment Interest:** Overdue amounts shall accrue interest at the rate of **1.5%** per month.

4.5 **Annual Price Adjustment (COLA):** Fees may be adjusted annually by the Consumer Price Index (CPI) increase, not to exceed **3%** per year.

## 6. SERVICE CREDITS AND PENALTIES

6.1 **Service Credit Calculation:** If Provider fails to meet any SLA target, Client shall be entitled to Service Credits as follows:

| SLA Achievement | Service Credit |
|-----------------|----------------|
| 99.0% - 99.49% | 5% of monthly fee |
| 98.0% - 98.99% | 10% of monthly fee |
| 97.0% - 97.99% | 15% of monthly fee |
| Below 97.0% | 25% of monthly fee |

6.2 **Maximum Credits:** Total Service Credits in any month shall not exceed **30%** of the monthly fee.

6.3 **Earn Back Provision:** Provider may earn back up to 50% of Service Credits by achieving 100% of all SLA targets for three consecutive months.

6.4 **Chronic Failure:** If Provider fails to meet any Critical SLA for three (3) consecutive months, Client may terminate this Agreement without penalty.

## 7. OBLIGATIONS

### 7.1 Provider Obligations

7.1.1 Provider shall maintain a dedicated account team including:
- Account Manager (available during business hours)
- Technical Lead (24/7 on-call)
- Service Delivery Manager

7.1.2 Provider shall deliver monthly performance reports by the **5th business day** of each month.

7.1.3 Provider shall conduct quarterly business reviews with Client stakeholders.

7.1.4 Provider shall maintain all required certifications including:
- ISO 27001 (Information Security)
- SOC 2 Type II (Security Controls)
- ITIL v4 Foundation

7.1.5 Provider shall notify Client within **24 hours** of any security incident.

7.1.6 Provider shall maintain business continuity plans with annual testing.

### 7.2 Client Obligations

7.2.1 Client shall provide reasonable access to systems and facilities.

7.2.2 Client shall designate a primary point of contact within **10 business days** of the Effective Date.

7.2.3 Client shall respond to Provider inquiries within **2 business days**.

7.2.4 Client shall provide timely approvals for change requests (within **5 business days**).

8.3 This obligation shall survive termination of this Agreement.

10.1 **Limitation of Liability:** Neither Party's total liability shall exceed **two times (2x)** the annual fees paid or payable under this Agreement.

10.2 **Exclusion of Damages:** Neither Party shall be liable for indirect, incidental, consequential, or punitive damages.

10.3 **Exceptions:** The limitations above shall not apply to:
- Breach of confidentiality obligations
- Intellectual property infringement
- Gross negligence or willful misconduct
- Indemnification obligations

## 13. TERMINATION

13.1 **Termination for Cause:** Either Party may terminate upon **30 days** written notice if the other Party materially breaches this Agreement and fails to cure within the notice period.

13.2 **Termination for Convenience:** Client may terminate for convenience upon **180 days** written notice, subject to payment of early termination fees.

13.3 **Early Termination Fees:**

| Termination Timing | Fee |
|--------------------|-----|
| Year 1 | 50% of remaining contract value |
| Year 2 | 35% of remaining contract value |
| Year 3 | 20% of remaining contract value |

13.4 **Transition Assistance:** Provider shall provide transition assistance for up to **90 days** following termination at then-current rates.
