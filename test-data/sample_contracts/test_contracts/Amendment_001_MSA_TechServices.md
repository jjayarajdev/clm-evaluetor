---

## 1. AMENDMENTS TO SCOPE OF SERVICES

### 1.1 Additional Services

Section 2 of the MSA is hereby amended to add the following services:

(a) **Cybersecurity Operations Center (SOC):** 24/7 security monitoring, threat detection, and incident response services.

(b) **DevOps Platform Management:** CI/CD pipeline management, container orchestration (Kubernetes), and infrastructure-as-code services.

(c) **Data Analytics Platform:** Management of data lake infrastructure, ETL processes, and business intelligence tools.

### 1.2 Expanded Geographic Coverage

Services shall now include Client's European operations, specifically:
- London, United Kingdom
- Frankfurt, Germany
- Amsterdam, Netherlands

## 5. NEW OBLIGATIONS

### 5.1 Provider Security Obligations

Provider shall:

(a) Maintain SOC 2 Type II certification for all new services;

(b) Conduct penetration testing quarterly (previously annually);

(c) Provide security awareness training to Client staff upon request;

(d) Maintain a dedicated Security Incident Response Team (SIRT);

(e) Provide monthly security posture reports by the **10th business day** of each month;

(f) Implement zero-trust security architecture within **6 months** of this Amendment's effective date.

### 5.2 Provider DevOps Obligations

Provider shall:

(a) Maintain container image vulnerability scanning;

(b) Implement GitOps practices for all infrastructure changes;

(c) Provide deployment rollback capability within **5 minutes**;

(d) Maintain separate development, staging, and production environments.

### 5.3 Client Obligations

Client shall:

(a) Designate a Security Liaison within **10 business days** of this Amendment's effective date;

(b) Provide timely access to European systems and facilities;

(c) Ensure compliance with GDPR for European operations;

(d) Participate in quarterly security reviews.
